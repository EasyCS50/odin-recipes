Creamy Rigatoni

Description

Try our Rigatoni pasta - this popular tube pasta is classically Italian and is wide enough to pair perfectly with Bolognese or your favorite sauce!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 1 (28-ounce) can whole peeled plum tomatoes, tomatoes crushed by hand and juices reserved
  • 12 ounces uncooked rigatoni pasta
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 ounce Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, for garnish

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a large Dutch oven over medium. Add onion; cook, stirring occasionally, until tender and golden brown, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and their juices; bring to a boil over medium.
  4. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer, stirring occasionally, until flavors meld and sauce thickens, about 30 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, cook pasta in salted water according to package directions for al dente. Drain pasta, reserving 1/2 cup pasta cooking liquid.
  6. Stir cream, salt, black pepper, and crushed red pepper into tomato sauce. Add cooked pasta and toss to coat.
  7. Add reserved pasta cooking liquid, 1/4 cup at a time, until desired consistency is reached.
  8. Divide pasta evenly among 4 bowls; sprinkle with Parmesan and garnish with parsley.
